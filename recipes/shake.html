Easy Morning Shake

Nutritious & Delicous Breakfast in Under 5 Minutes!

“If You Fail to Plan, You Are Planning to Fail” — Benjamin Franklin

Like so many of us, my mornings can be hectic trying to get in exercise, planning my workday, taking care my dog and getting a kid to school on time. I don't always have the time or desire to fix a proper breakfast. This is a recipe for a shake that from start to finish takes 5 minutes. I know because I have timed it. And it tastes delicous!

So keep reading if you have mornings like mine!

Ingredients List (Serves 1)

  • 8 oz Unsweetened Almond Milk (cow's milk, oat milk, and soy milk are also fine)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate flavored whey Protein
  • 2 Tbs PB Fit Peanut butter Protein Powder
  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen berries
  • 1 cup of frozen spinach

The Steps

  1. Place the banana, whey, PB Fit, spinach and berries in your bullet blender
  2. Pour almond milk in last
  3. Blend until smooth

And that is it! You can drink it as you get ready for work, slam it before you go out the door, or drink during your commute.
